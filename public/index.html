FAQ

Is this free?

Yes — completely free, no account required.

What formats are supported?

MP4 video (144p, 360p, 720p, 1080p, 1440p) and high-quality MP3 audio.

Can I download playlists?

Yes! Paste a YouTube playlist URL and you can download individual videos or all videos at once in MP4 or MP3 format.

Is downloading YouTube videos legal?

Downloading for personal offline viewing of content you have rights to is generally permitted. Always respect copyright law and YouTube's Terms of Service.

Why does the highest quality show separately?

YouTube's 1080p+ streams are sometimes audio-only or video-only. We badge the best combined stream so you always know what you're getting.

What is the MP3 quality?

We provide the highest-quality audio stream available from YouTube, typically 128-256 kbps depending on the video source.
