FAQ

Is this free?

Yes — completely free, no account required.

What formats are supported?

MP4 (video+audio), WebM, and MP3 audio-only streams, depending on what YouTube provides.

Is downloading YouTube videos legal?

Downloading for personal, offline viewing of content you have rights to is generally permitted. Redistributing copyrighted content is not. Always respect copyright law and YouTube's Terms of Service.

Why does the highest quality show separately?

YouTube's 1080p+ streams are audio-only or video-only. We badge the best combined stream and the highest raw stream separately so you always know what you're getting.
